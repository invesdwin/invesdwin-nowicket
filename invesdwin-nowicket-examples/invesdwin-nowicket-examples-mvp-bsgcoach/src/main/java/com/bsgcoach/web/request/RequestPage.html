How to export the CSV reports from BSG-Online:

  1. Login to your Decisions/Reports screen.
  2. Click on Export Options & Data Reset at the bottom of the menu.
  3. Export the three reports (FIR, CIR, COR) for the given Year you want feedback for.
  4. Finally, Upload the files here to get your feedback!
